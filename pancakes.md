# Pancakes 

- Buy pancakes from the store. 

## Ingredients 

- Eggs
- Flour 
- Water
- Salt 
- Sugar
- Milk 
- Butter 

## Preparing the batter 

- Combine the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. 
- Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and butter. 
- Throw it all away. 
- Buy pancakes from the store. 
- Heat them in the microwave. 
- Done.
